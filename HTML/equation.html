Equations

Equations are using multiplications and divisions. If you did not do the lecture on it, you should do them ! click here for multiplication and here for divisions

Equations exist to find numbers in different situation. Example

You have 10 apples selling for 15.4€and you would like to know how much is one apple. Let 'x' be the price for one apple. You know that x*10 = 15.4. Then, x = 1.54. One apple is 1.54€. But how did I found this number

An equation is an equality. 10=a*5, 50 = 3*b etc. The left part is equal to the right part
